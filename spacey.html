Spacey: Tonal Gravity

This is a project I did where I used Canvas, FabricJS and PhysicsJS to simulate bodies in an outer space environment.
There is a proximity equation that measures distances between bodies using Pythagorian priciples. I used WebAudio to dynamically create
sounds that represent the force of gravity. Each body has it's own oscillator and plugged into the custom audio graph.
There are hand crafted Convolver, Distortion and Delay effect algorithms included in the audio graph. I wrote a series about the
process and construction of the WebAudio elements on Medium
